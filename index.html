How to Play
🍳
Drag the Pan Tap or drag anywhere on screen to move the frying pan. It follows your finger.
🥟
Keep it Alive The dumpling bounces around the kitchen. Hit it with the pan before it escapes!
💀
Two Ways to Die The dumpling dies if it falls off the bottom or flies off the top. Left and right walls bounce it back safely.
⭐
Score Points Each pan hit = 1 point. Obstacles appear after 5 hits to mix things up. Speed increases the longer you survive!
🔓
Unlock More Beat your high score to unlock new foods. Play more games to unlock new restaurants!
